How We Work

InterviewMate is a platform where students can share their experiences during on-campus interview. Students can search questions based on their schools, post their questions, and answer others' questions

Join and select your school

Selecting your school let us better help you find the most common questions asked on your campus

Post your experiences or search for others

When preparing interviews, you can easily search for questions asked just recently on your campus. After interview, you can post your experience here

Lead a discussion

Want to hear fellow students' idea about a coding question? Join our discussion board and post your solution!
